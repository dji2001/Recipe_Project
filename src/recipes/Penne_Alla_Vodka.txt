Recipe Name : Penne Alla Vodka
Description: Yummy pasta dish that only takes 25 minutes to make and serves 4 people.
Ingredients: 
1: 1 pound of penne
2: 3 cloves of garlic, minced
3: 1 whole medium onion, chopped
4: 3 tablespoons of butter
5: 2 tablespoons of olive oil
6: 1 cup of vodka
7: one 14 ounce can of tomato sauce
8: 1 cup of heavy cream
9: grated parmesan
10: DONE
Instructions:
1: Saute the garlic and onions in 2 tablespoons butter and the olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat until soft.
2: Pour in the vodka, being careful if your stove has an open flame (remove the pan from the heat before adding).
3: Cook and reduce for 2 to 3 minutes, then pour in the tomato puree.
4: Stir the mixture until it's thoroughly combined, then reduce the heat to low.
5: Pour in the heavy cream. Stir to combine, then turn heat to the lowest simmer possible. 
6: Pour the drained pasta into the sauce and toss to combine. Sprinkle on the Parmesan and enjoy!
