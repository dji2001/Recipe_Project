Recipe Name : Mojito
Description: A nice and refreshing drink for a hot summer day, but only if you're 21+!
Ingredients: 
1: 10 fresh mint leaves
2: ½ lime, cut into 4 wedges
3: 2 tablespoons white sugar, or to taste
4: 1 cup ice cubes
5: 1 ½ fluid ounces white rum
6: ½ cup club soda
7: DONE
Instructions: 
1: Place mint leaves and 1 lime wedge into a sturdy glass.
2: Use a muddler to crush the mint and lime to release the mint oils and lime juice. 
3: Add 2 more lime wedges and the sugar, and muddle again to release the lime juice. 
4: Fill the glass almost to the top with ice. 
5: Stir, taste, and add more sugar if desired. Garnish with the remaining lime wedge.
6: DONE
